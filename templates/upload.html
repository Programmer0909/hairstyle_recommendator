Heart

Heart-shaped faces: wide forehead, narrow chin. Versatile hairstyles, from bangs to waves.

Round

Full cheeks, rounded chin. Styles with volume on top elongate the face, while soft layers add definition.

Oblong

Elongated with balanced width. Go for short to medium lengths with layers to create width and avoid excessive length.
